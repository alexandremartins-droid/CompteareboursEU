Mission: Connected Heroes

Important – Registration Required

To become a true Connected Hero, registration is mandatory. Without completing the official Registration Form, you will not be able to access the challenges, earn points, or appear in the regional rankings.

This step is essential to validate your identity, connect you to your region, and unlock your access to the Mission: Connected Heroes platform.

Only registered participants can:

• Access the 3 sessions of The Academy
• Join the interactive journey of The Practice Excursion
• Submit their creative vision in The Laboratory
• Qualify for The Big Finale
• Earn rewards and appear in the official ranking
