Article 1 — Organizing Company
STELLANTIS Auto SAS, a simplified joint-stock company with a capital of 300 176 800 euros, registered with the Versailles Trade and Companies Register under number B 542 065 479, whose registered office is located at 43 Rue Jean Pierre Timbaud – 78300 Poissy, is organizing from 8 December 2025 to March 14th 2026 included, a game with no obligation to purchase entitled “Mission Connected Heroes” (hereinafter referred to as the “Game”).
Article 2 — Game Duration and Description
The Game will take place from 8 December 2025 to March 14th 2026 included.

The Game “Mission: Connected Heroes” is a unifying global initiative bringing together more than 22,000 STELLANTIS employees from the Product Development and Technology Business Unit (PDT) across four strategic regions (North America, Europe, South America, and IAP/China). Its aim is to create a shared culture and harmonize knowledge around connected services.
Article 3 — Definitions
Participant: Internal Stellantis employee meeting eligibility conditions.
Challenge: Multiple-choice or open-question activities required to progress.
SharePoint: Platform hosting the Game for each region.
Article 4 — Challenge Objectives
The Game has four objectives:
  • Unite participants from 4 regions around a common, engaging program
  • Clarify and give meaning to the Group's connected services
  • Train the best internal ambassadors
  • Strengthen pride and belonging through a fun and digital experience
Article 5 — Participation Conditions
5.1 Eligibility
Only internal STELLANTIS employees with permanent contracts under PDT, from the beginning of the challenge, are eligible. External contractors are excluded.

5.2 Language
The official language of the Challenge is English.

5.3 Registration and Participation
Participation is voluntary and individual. Access is via SharePoint with a Stellantis username and password. Participation constitutes full acceptance of these rules.

Article 7 — Game Calendar

Article 8 — Game Journey
The Academy: 3 learning sessions (10–15 min).
The Practice: Interactive logistics simulation with choice-based progression.
The Laboratory: Open creativity challenge (text).
The Big Finale: Original content creation + voting.
Article 9 — Point System
Mission 1: +1 / –1, min 0, timed bonus.
Mission 2: 1–3 pts depending on choices + bonus.
Mission 3: +1 if answer provided.
Finale: Votes-based ranking + tie-breaking rules.
Article 10 — Prizes
Gold Prizes: F1 Grand Prix or equivalent trips (region specific).
Silver Prizes: Connected services subscription or connected speaker.
Bronze Prizes: Tote bag + notebook + water bottle.

All prizes are nominative and non-transferable.
