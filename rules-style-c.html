Mission: Connected Heroes

Official Rules

Article 1: Organizing Company

STELLANTIS Auto SAS, a simplified joint-stock company with a capital of 300 176 800 euros, registered with the Versailles Trade and Companies Register under number B 542 065 479, whose registered office is located at 43 Rue Jean Pierre Timbaud – 78300 Poissy, is organizing from 8 December 2025 to March 14th, 2026 included, a game with no obligation to purchase entitled “Mission Connected Heroes” (hereinafter referred to as the “Game”).

Article 2: Game Duration and Global Description

The game will take place from 8 December 2025 to March 14th, 2026 included.

The Game “Mission: Connected Heroes” is a unifying global initiative that aims to bring together more than 22,000 STELLANTIS employees from the Product Development and Technology Business Unit (PDT). This challenge, deployed simultaneously in North America, Europe, South America, and IAP/China, aims to create a common culture and harmonize knowledge around connected services.

Article 3: Definitions

  • “Participant”: Individual who participates in the Game by meeting the participation conditions and who is an employee of a company belonging to Stellantis Group.
  • “Challenge”: Multiple-choice questionnaire or open questions to which Participants must respond.
  • “SharePoint”: Platform on which the Game is hosted for each region.

Article 4: Challenge Objectives

  • Unite participants across 4 regions around a common and engaging program.
  • Clarify the connected services offering.
  • Train the best ambassadors for connected services.
  • Engage teams through a fun and digital experience.

Article 5: Participation Conditions

5.1 Eligibility
Reserved for internal STELLANTIS employees under PDT with a permanent contract for the full duration of the challenge. External providers are excluded.

5.2 Language
The official language of the challenge is English.

5.3 Registration
Participation is voluntary. One participation per person. Connection is made with the STELLANTIS Auto SAS username and password.
The Organizing Company may terminate participation without notice for non-compliance.

Article 6: Personal Data Protection

6.1 Data Collected

  • First/last name
  • Professional email
  • Stellantis identifier
  • Entity/department
  • Region and country
  • Connection & participation data
  • Content created during the challenge

6.2 Purposes

  • Challenge management
  • Winner determination & tax management
  • Anonymized statistical analysis
  • Internal communication
  • Improving connected services knowledge

6.3 Legal Basis

  • Legitimate interest of STELLANTIS Auto SAS
  • Execution of these rules

  • Legal obligations

6.4 Retention Period
Personal data kept until 3 months after prize-giving, then deleted or anonymized.

6.5 Recipients

  • Organizing teams
  • Technical service providers
  • Bailiff (limited data)

  • No transfer outside EEA without safeguards

Right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and post-death data instructions.

STELLANTIS Auto SAS implements appropriate technical & organizational security measures.

Article 7: Game Calendar

  • Week 50 2025: Mission 1 (Dec 8, 15, 22)

  • Week 09: “THE BIG FINALE”

Article 8: Game Journey

8.1 Educational Phase (The Academy)
Three sessions (10–15 min each) covering:

  • Drive Smarter
  • Life Easier
  • Trip Enjoyable

8.2 Practical Phase (The Practice)
Interactive logistics journey with choices influencing the path.

8.3 Creative Phase (The Laboratory)
Open question on future connected services.

8.4 Finale (The Big Finale)
Creation of content (video, audio, scenario, visual). Top performers qualify per region (200 EU, 100 NA, 35 SA, 35 IAP/China). Voting determines winners.

Article 9: Point System

Mission 1

  • Correct answer: +1
  • Incorrect: –1
  • Score cannot go below 0
  • Timed bonus

Mission 2

  • 1–3 pts per decision
  • +2 pts if completed on time
  • +1 pt if completed late
  • 0 pts if failed
  • Open-ended questions: full completion = 1 pt

Mission 3
Answer provided = 1 point

Grand Finale
Ranking based on votes, then previous points, then completion time, then bailiff-supervised draw.
