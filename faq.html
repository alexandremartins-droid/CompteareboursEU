SECTION 01
ELIGIBILITY
What is "Mission: Connected Heroes"?
"Mission: Connected Heroes" is an ambitious program designed to unite over 22,000 employees across four major Stellantis regions around connected services. It's a fun, digital experience inspired by gaming and superhero themes that will help you become an expert in Stellantis connected services.
When does the challenge take place?
The challenge timeline is as follows:

December 2025: Mission 1 "THE ACADEMY" with 3 challenges (8th, 15th, 22nd)
January 12–23rd, 2026: Mission 2 "THE PRACTICE"
February 2nd, 2026: Mission 3 "THE LABORATORY" begins
February 16th, 2026: THE BIG FINALE starts
Mid-March 2026: DIGITAL CEREMONY to announce winners
Who can participate in the challenge?
All internal Stellantis employees working under the PDT - Product Development and Technology entity are eligible, except external collaborators and non-permanent contracts.

If someone joins PDT during the game, they cannot play unless their contract covers the entire duration of the challenge.
Is participation mandatory?
No, participation is voluntary.
What language will be used for the challenge?
The superhero community language is English. All main communications and the platform will be in English.
How do I register for the challenge?
You don’t need to register — the challenge takes place directly on your Microsoft environment via your regional SharePoint site.
Do I need any special equipment to participate?
You will need a computer with internet access. The game is designed for SharePoint and not recommended for smartphones or tablets. VPN access may be required.
When should I play?
You may play during or outside working hours depending on local HR policies. The platform remains accessible outside working hours.
If I am not available during a mission's opening period?
Missions 1–3 remain open until the final closing date of Mission 3 ("The Laboratory").
SECTION 02
GAMEPLAY & MISSIONS
How does the challenge work?
The challenge consists of four sequential phases:

1. THE ACADEMY — Learn the basics of Connected Services
2. THE PRACTICE — A logistics journey simulation in an interactive course
3. THE LABORATORY — Creative challenge imagining future Connected Services
4. THE BIG FINALE — For top performers only, including content creation and voting
How do I access the challenge?
Through the dedicated SharePoint platform using your Stellantis username and password.
Can I participate as a team?
No, participation is individual only.
SECTION 03
PERSONAL DATA
What will happen to my personal data?
Your data will not be permanently recorded. It will be used only to select winners and processed according to Stellantis’ privacy policy and GDPR regulations.
SECTION 04
POINTS & RANKING
How does the points system work?
✔ Correct answer = +1 point
✔ Incorrect answer = –1 point
✔ Minimum score = 0
✔ Some challenges include timed bonus points
How can I check my ranking?
A ranking for your region and country will be visible on the SharePoint platform, along with your personal position.
How many participants advance to the finale?
• First 200 in Europe (EE)
• First 100 in North America (NA)
• First 35 in South America (SA)
• First 35 in India Asia Pacific / China (IAP/China)
SECTION 05
PRIZES & REWARDS
When and how will winners be announced?
During a global digital ceremony in mid-March 2026.
Is the contest fair for all participants?
Yes — fairness is ensured by a bailiff overseeing rule integrity throughout the challenge.
When and how will the prizes be awarded?
Prizes will be awarded by the SWX regional marketing department.
I won the first prize but I am unavailable. Can I transfer it?
Yes, you may invite the person of your choice if you are unavailable.
Will I have to declare the winnings for taxes?
Tax obligations vary by region and country. Consult your regional HR department.
SECTION 06
COMMUNICATION & SUPPORT
What if I encounter technical issues?
Contact your regional SharePoint and technical support teams.
What if I miss a mission due to vacation or work commitments?
Missions are accessible during their active period. Missing a date may impact your ranking.
Where can I find more information about connected services?
A media library with documentation will be available on the SharePoint platform.
Who can I contact if I have questions not covered here?
• For access issues: Contact your internal Stellantis IT support.
• For organization questions: Check this FAQ or contact the organizing team via the platform form.
• For connected services issues: Contact your local vehicle brand call center.
