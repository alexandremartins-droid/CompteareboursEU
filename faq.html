SECTION 01
ELIGIBILITY
What is "Mission: Connected Heroes"?
"Mission: Connected Heroes" is an ambitious program designed to unite over 22,000 employees across four major Stellantis regions around connected services. It's a fun, digital experience inspired by gaming and superhero themes that will help you become an expert in Stellantis connected services.
When does the challenge take place?
December 2025: Mission 1 "THE ACADEMY" with 3 challenges (8th, 15th, 22nd)
January 12-23rd, 2026: Mission 2 "THE PRACTICE"
February 2nd, 2026: Mission 3 "THE LABORATORY" begins
February 16th, 2026: THE BIG FINALE starts
Mid-March 2026: DIGITAL CEREMONY to announce winners
Who can participate in the challenge?
All internal Stellantis employees working under the PDT entity are eligible except external collaborators and non-permanent contracts. New PDT arrivals cannot play unless their contract covers the entire challenge duration.
Is participation mandatory?
No. Participation is voluntary.
What language will be used?
All communications and the platform will be in English.
How do I register?
No registration needed — everything happens on your regional SharePoint.
Do I need any special equipment?
A computer with internet access and VPN. Mobile devices not recommended.
When should I play?
Whenever you want — during or outside working hours depending on regional HR rules.
If I am not available during a mission…?
You have until the closing date of Mission 3 to complete Missions 1–3.
SECTION 02
GAMEPLAY & MISSIONS
How does the challenge work?
The challenge works across four phases:

1. THE ACADEMY: Educational connected services basics
2. THE PRACTICE: Simulation-based practical challenges
3. THE LABORATORY: Creative design challenges
4. THE BIG FINALE: Top performer content creation with votes
How do I access the challenge?
Through your regional SharePoint using your Stellantis credentials.
Can I participate as a team?
No. Individual participation only.
SECTION 03
PERSONAL DATA
What will happen to my personal data?
The data is not permanently stored and is used only to select winners, in compliance with privacy and GDPR regulations.
SECTION 04
POINTS & RANKING
How does the points system work?
✔ Correct answer = +1 point
✔ Incorrect answer = –1 point
✔ Minimum = 0
✔ Some challenges include bonus points for fastest participants
How can I check my ranking?
Regional & country leaderboards will be available after each stage.
How many participants advance to the finale?
Europe: 200
North America: 100
South America: 35
India Asia Pacific/China: 35
SECTION 05
PRIZES & REWARDS
When and how will winners be announced?
During a digital ceremony in mid-March 2026.
Is the contest fair?
Yes. A bailiff ensures fairness and rule integrity.
How and when will prizes be awarded?
By the SWX regional marketing department.
Can I transfer my prize?
Yes, if you cannot attend, you may designate someone else.
Do I have to declare winnings for taxes?
Tax obligations vary by region. Contact HR for details.

TECHNICAL SUPPORT
What if I encounter technical issues?
Contact your regional SharePoint & technical support.
What if I miss a mission?
You can complete earlier missions until Mission 3 closes.
Where can I find more info about connected services?
A media library will be available on SharePoint.
Who can I contact for questions?
➤ Technical access: Internal Stellantis IT support
➤ Game organization: Contact the team via the SharePoint form
