Section 01

Eligibility

? What is "Mission: Connected Heroes"?

"Mission: Connected Heroes" is an ambitious program designed to unite over 22,000 employees across four major Stellantis regions around connected services. It's a fun, digital experience inspired by gaming and superhero themes that will help you become an expert in Stellantis connected services.

? When does the challenge take place?

The challenge timeline is as follows:

  • December 2025: Mission 1 “THE ACADEMY” with 3 challenges (8th, 15th, 22nd)
  • January 12–23rd, 2026: Mission 2 “THE PRACTICE”
  • February 2nd, 2026: Mission 3 “THE LABORATORY” begins
  • February 16th, 2026: “THE BIG FINALE” starts
  • Mid-March 2026: Digital ceremony to announce winners
? Who can participate in the challenge?

All internal Stellantis employees working under the PDT – Product Development and Technology entity are eligible to participate, except for external collaborators and non-permanent contracts.

If someone arrives at the PDT department during the game, they cannot play. They can only play if their contract covers the entire duration of the challenge.

? Is participation mandatory?

No, participation is voluntary. The challenge is designed to be engaging and fun, but it remains optional.

? What language will be used for the challenge?

The Superhero community language is English. All main communications and the platform will be in English.

? How do I register for the challenge?

You don’t need to register, because the challenge takes place on your Microsoft environment, through the dedicated SharePoint site for your region. The link will be shared via email at the beginning of December.

? Do I need any special equipment to participate?

You will need access to a computer with an internet connection. The game is designed to be played on the SharePoint platform only, and it is not recommended to use smartphones or tablets.

Remember that you will need an internet connection and VPN access.

? When should I play?

The challenge is open both during and outside of working hours. It's entirely up to you. Specific arrangements may vary by region and country, in accordance with local HR policies.

The platform remains accessible outside of working hours if you prefer to participate at a more relaxed time.

? If I am not available during a mission's opening period, how long do I have to complete it?

The first three missions remain open even after subsequent missions have started. Therefore, you have until the closing date of Mission 3, entitled “The Laboratory”, to complete them.

Section 02

Gameplay & Missions

? How does the challenge work?

The challenge consists of three main missions followed by a finale:

  • 1. THE ACADEMY: Educational challenges focusing on the basics of Connected Services.
  • 2. THE PRACTICE: Practical challenges where the participant is placed in a simulation of a long-distance logistics journey, presented as an interactive course.
  • 3. THE LABORATORY: Creative challenges where you imagine future Connected Services.
  • 4. THE BIG FINALE: For top performers only, includes content creation and a vote from everyone.
? How do I access the challenge?

You can access the challenge through the dedicated SharePoint platform using your Stellantis username and password.

? Can I participate as a team?

No, the challenge is designed for individual participation only. There is one participation per person, and no teams.

Section 03

Personal Data

? What will happen to my personal data shared in connection with this event?

The data will not be permanently recorded. It will only be used for the purpose of selecting the winners and will be processed in accordance with Stellantis' privacy policy and applicable GDPR regulations.

Section 04

Points & Ranking

? How does the points system work?
  • Each correct answer earns you 1 point.
  • Each incorrect answer deducts 1 point.
  • The minimum score is 0.
  • Some challenges will be timed, and the fastest participants will earn bonus points.
? How can I check my ranking?

At each stage, a ranking by region and country will be available on the SharePoint platform, along with your personal ranking.

? How many participants will advance to the finale?

Employees with the highest number of points will advance to the finale:

  • First 200 in Europe (EE)
  • First 100 in North America (NA)
  • First 35 in South America (SA)
  • First 35 in India Asia Pacific/China (IAP/China)
Section 05

Prizes & Rewards

? When and how will winners be announced?

Winners will be announced during a global digital ceremony in mid-March 2026.

? Is the contest fair for all participants?

Yes, fairness is guaranteed as all heroes have the same chances. A bailiff ensures justice and the integrity of the rules throughout the challenge.

? When and how will the prizes be awarded?

The first prizes will be awarded in due course by the SWX regional marketing department, as will the second and third prizes.

? I won the first prize, but I am not available. Can I transfer my prize to the person of my choice?

Yes, if you are unavailable, you are free to invite the person(s) of your choice.

? Will I have to declare the winnings to my government and pay taxes on the amount of the first prizes?

Tax obligations vary by region and country. Please consult your regional HR department for specific information regarding the tax implications of received prizes.

Section 06

Technical Support

? What if I encounter technical issues during the challenge?

If you encounter technical issues, you can contact your regional SharePoint and technical support teams. Contact information will be available on the SharePoint platform.

? What if I miss a mission due to vacation or work commitments?

The missions are designed to be completed at your convenience within their active periods. If you miss a specific challenge date, you may still be able to participate in subsequent missions, but this might affect your overall ranking.

? Where can I find more information about connected services?

A media library with information about connected services will be available on the SharePoint platform. This will include training materials and documentation specific to your region.

Section 07

Communication & Support

? How will I receive updates about the challenge?

Updates will be communicated through the SharePoint platform, Teams notifications, and possibly through your regional communication channels.

? Who can I contact if I have questions not covered in this FAQ?

The SharePoint platform will include a dedicated FAQ section and contact information for support. You can reach out to the organizing team through these channels.

  • For difficulties accessing the SharePoint platform: contact your internal Stellantis IT support.
  • For questions about the organization of the challenge: consult the dedicated FAQ page on the SharePoint platform. If you cannot find your answer there, you can contact an organizing representative via the form available on the platform.
  • If you experience difficulties with a connected service: contact the call center of the vehicle brand available in your country.
